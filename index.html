Our Story

Dave's Restaurant is a family run restaurant, originating from the valleys of South Wales. We started from family dinners with Nan's secret recipes to an award winning restuarant. Our famous Chef Lucien was trained in France and worked in Michelin starred restaurants for over a decade. So please come and enjoy his home cooked food!

We pride ourselves on serving great quality food and drink, and we can promise great value… and of course, a generous serving of sizzle! So, whether you’re looking for somewhere to enjoy a meal with friends and family, watch the footy with a few mates over an iced cold beer or to enjoy your next celebration, you’ll find that Dave's Restaurant suits you.
